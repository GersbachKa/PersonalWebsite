All About My Research

My current research involves the search for gravitational waves from Super Massive Black Holes (SMBHs) using a galaxy sized gravitational wave detector called a Pulsar Timing Array (PTA). I am currently working as a associate member for the North American Nanohertz Observatory for Gravitational waves (NANOGrav).
